## 📖 About
**Siggy Temple** is a high-performance, responsive Telegram Mini App (TMA). It’s an addictive gaming ecosystem where you gather **Prayers** to climb the spiritual hierarchy. Featuring arcade games, gambling, and a mysterious floating confessional.

## 🌟 Features

### 🙏 1. The Temple (Clicker)
* Tap the Siggy avatar to generate core Prayers.
* Energy system (1000/1000) with auto-regen.
* Haptic-like visual feedback and floating text.

### 🪙 2. Head or Tail
* High-stakes gambling with 3D coin flip animations.
* Double your prayers or lose it all to the gods.

### 😺 3. Flappy Siggy
* Skill-based arcade mode. Navigate through obstacles.
* Earn passive prayer flow for every obstacle cleared.

### 🧠 4. Ritual Quiz
* Lore-based challenges. Prove your devotion.
* Massive rewards for correct answers (+1,000 Prayers).

### 🛒 5. The Store
* **Multipliers**: x10 (30m) or x100 (1m) power-ups.
* **Loyalist Cert**: The ultimate badge of honor (1M Prayers).

### ⛪ 6. The Confessional
* Floating chat system to confess your sins.
* Costs 1,000 Prayers. Random spiritual guidance and stickers.

Optimized for **Telegram Mini Apps**:
- **Native Theme**: Supports Telegram's header/background color syncing.
